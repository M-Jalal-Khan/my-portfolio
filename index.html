About Me

I’m not here to just analyze data — I’m here to make it count. With over three years of experience in data analytics, business intelligence, and machine learning, I help organizations move from disconnected numbers to clear, confident decisions. My background spans freelance consulting and in-house BI transformation, supporting leadership teams with reporting systems, predictive models, and dynamic dashboards that lead to measurable improvements—not just more information.

My work is rooted in practical outcomes. I’ve built automated reporting pipelines that save teams dozens of hours each month. I’ve designed dashboards that surface what matters most—without overwhelming the people who rely on them. And I’ve delivered machine learning solutions that help businesses stay ahead of challenges, not react to them after the fact.

What sets me apart is a simple but important principle: every data solution should serve a purpose. I build with intention—thinking not just about how the system works, but who it’s for, how it will be used, and what decisions it needs to support. My strength lies in blending technical expertise with a strategic mindset, so data becomes not just accessible, but actionable.

Data should simplify decisions, not complicate them. That’s the standard I build for—and it’s the value I deliver.
