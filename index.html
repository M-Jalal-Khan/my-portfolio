About Me

I am Muhammad Jalal Khan, a Strategic Data & Business Intelligence Consultant with a passion for transforming raw data into compelling narratives and actionable insights. My journey in data began with a fascination for uncovering hidden patterns and has evolved into a mission to drive measurable business growth through intelligent data solutions.

With a robust background in Power BI, Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn), SQL, and advanced analytics tools, I specialize in building end-to-end data pipelines, developing predictive models, and crafting interactive dashboards that empower stakeholders to make informed decisions. I thrive on solving complex business problems, whether it's optimizing supply chains, predicting customer churn, or enhancing operational efficiency.

My approach is always client-centric: I focus on understanding your unique challenges and translating them into data-driven strategies that deliver tangible results. I believe that data, when properly harnessed, is the most powerful asset a business can possess. Let's connect and explore how we can leverage your data to achieve your strategic objectives.
